Brian Kim is an assistant professor of materials science and engineering and physics at the University of Arizona. He received his PhD in electrical engineering from Stanford University in 2018 and was a postdoctoral research scientist at Columbia University from 2018 to 2023. He is the recipient of the Outstanding Young Researcher Award (2024), jointly sponsored by the Association of Korean Physicists in America and the Korean Physical Society. He is also a member of APL Photonics Early Career Editorial Advisory Board (2025-26), and Scilog Fellow in Quantum Matter and Information sponsored by RCSA (2025-2027).
